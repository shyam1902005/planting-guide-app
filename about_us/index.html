Welcome to Planting Guide

Discover how our project empowers gardeners to grow smarter and more sustainably.

Our Mission

We aim to revolutionize how people plant by providing accurate plant recommendations tailored to the environment. Whether you're a beginner gardener or an experienced grower, our guide helps you make informed decisions about what to plant and when.

Our Journey

Started as a small project by a group of students, the Planting Guide has grown into a full-fledged platform connecting people with the right plants for their region. With a focus on sustainability, we strive to reduce plant failure and promote greener living.

Why Choose Planting Guide?

Our guide is more than just a planting tool. We care deeply about your success as a gardener and the health of our environment. By selecting the right plants for your climate and soil, we aim to reduce water waste and ensure long-term plant health.
